Background & Vision

The StoreHouse is a local, Grand Rapids based non-profit. The organization seeks to obtain products from both local and national donors in order to distribute them to other non-profits and communities in need. Currently, they have no effective way of tracking their inventory, which is very diverse and frequently changing in composition.

Our vision is to provide The StoreHouse with a prototype web-based interface that shows aspects of their current inventory. We are aiming to improve their ability to efficiently receive, organize, and distribute items in a way that is not technologically overwhelming for the organizationâ€™s employees and volunteers.
